- For an Account Executive role, lead with Opportunity, TCO, Configurator, and MLOps.
- For a technical-sales or solutions-consulting role, lead with Configurator, Capacity, MLOps, and TCO.
- For a Google Cloud role, lead with GCP Data, MLOps, Opportunity, and TCO.
- Keep a one-page resume to four projects; link this portfolio for the complete seven-project system.
- Describe the three foundation repositories as sanitized blueprints, not deployable public applications.
- Describe commercial-workbench outputs as fictional decision-support demonstrations, not customer outcomes or vendor quotes.
